Dr. Jamal Saleh

Senior Consultant Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr.Jamal Saleh

Dr. Saleh practiced orthopaedics for over 25 years. Having left Bahrain Defence Forces Hospital as their leading orthopaedic surgeon, Dr. Saleh started Orthocare with a team of international specialists, physiotherapist, and medical staff. Dr. Saleh is a member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons, Director of ATLS and also function as the President of Bahrain Osteoporosis Society and past president of BAH sports medicine association.
