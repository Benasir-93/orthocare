SERVICES

On-Site Therapy

Patients can avail of an On-Site Physical Therapy and rehabilitation service. Our Physical Therapies can design an individual exercise program for each patient so that optimum recovery is achieved as soon as possible. .

This approach results in lower therapy utilization and reduced lost time or modified duty days.
Employees Health & Safety

To help maintain a fit work force from all walks of life, our team works in planned stages. Our range is comprehensive, from diagnosing work-related injuries, including cumulative trauma caused by repetitive injuries, to helping injured staff recover and return to their work place safely.

Sports Medicine

Whether you are an injured professional athlete or just want to be able to walk again without pain, our Physicians will do their best to help you achieve your goal.

Our Physicians are trained in the field of sports medicine and orthopedics. They are experts at diagnosing and treating orthopedic and sports medicine problems. In addition, we have the latest reconstructive techniques, advanced bracing mechanisms, effective therapeutic techniques to help patients resume a full range of activities.

Physiotherapy

We usually recommend movement and exercise to help improve the patient’s mobility, restore function and well-being. Helps through physical rehabilitation after an injury or surgery to return the activities of daily living in the shortest possible time, and to prevent further injury.

Common conditions requiring Physiotherapy:
  • Achilles tendonitis,Achilles Tendon Repair
  • ACL Reconstruction
  • Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (Gehrigs’s Ds)
  • Back and Neck Pain and Foot pain
  • Cancer
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Motor Vehicle Accident
  • Spinal Stenosis
  • Meniscal Tears of the Knee
  • Neurological Conditions
  • Tennis or Golfer’s Elbow

...
Our techniques used that may help to reduce pain, promote healing, and improve function include:

  • Electrotherapy (TENS, EMS, Interferential)
  • Ultrasound, Microwave/Shortwave Diathermy
  • Shockwave Therapy, Traction
  • Hot and Cold applications (ice/hot packs, paraffin wax)
  • Manual Therapy (mobilization, soft tissue release)
  • Exercise Therapy
  • Gait and Balance Training
  • Splinting, Bracing, and Taping
  • Device Fitting
