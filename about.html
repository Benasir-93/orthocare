ABOUT US

Welcome to OrthoCare Clinic

Orthocare Centre is one of the most specialized orthopedic clinics in the Middle East. The facility was envisioned by the distinguished orthopedic surgeon,Dr. Jamal Saleh as an advanced center for treating all kinds of orthopedic conditions. Dr. Saleh practiced orthopedics for over 25 years. Having left Bahrain Defence Forces Hospital as their leading orthopedic surgeon, Dr. Saleh started Orthocare with a team of international specialists, physiotherapist, and medical staff. Dr. Saleh is a member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons, Director of ATLS and also function as the President of Bahrain Osteoporosis Society and past president of BAH sports medicine association.
